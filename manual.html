Importance

The medicine manual contains the composition, cautions, and taking method etc.
Because there are too many kinds of medicine, method to use medicine are various.
Therefore, we should read the medicine manual to know information of medicine that we will take.


Contents

  • Composition
    Describe the composition and shape of the medicine.

  • Efficacy
    Describe the effects of this medicine, applicable diseases.

  • Taking method
    Describe how much you should eat, such as once dose and total daily dose.

  • Cautions
    A description of the possible side effects of this medicine.
    A description of cases that should not be taken and cases that should be taken with care.

  • Date of use
    A description of the available time frame when properly stowed in packaged state.

  • Stop taking
    A description of symptoms that you should stop taking and need to be discussed with specialist.

  • Storage method
    A description of appropriate storage methods to maintain the efficacy of medicine.


Writing rules

  • Composition
    If certain effective ingredients(acetaminophen or Nonsteroidal Antiinflammatory Drugs) are included, it is recommended that
    the name of the ingredient be highlighted (shown on a yellow background)
    so that consumers can pay attention to the risk of misuse, such as taking duplicate doses.

  • Efficacy
    Based on the safety and effectiveness of the product from the results of properly planned and well controlled clinical trials.

  • Taking method
    Capacity is clearly described according to the administration method, path, the effectiveness, and the intended patient.

  • Cautions
    Cautions should be marked in bold lines, and the background should be yellow so that consumers can check first.
