Promotion Video

This video is about Thalidomide.
Thalidomide has been marketed as a sedative, sleeping pill that can be purchased without a doctor's prescription.
However, almost 12,000 babies were born with short or no arms and legs by pregnant woman had Thalidomide.

The Thalidomide incident made people realize risk of side effects, and caused the Kefauver-Harris Amendment.
Additionally, in U.S., DR.Kelsey claimed not to bring in Thalidomide, because researches of medicine were lacked.
As a result, the U.S. was able to avoid disaster.


Development Background

Despite the medicine involved in our daily lives, we were not usually aware of side effects of medicine or how to take them correctly.
The wrong medicine intake causes very severe side effects, and endangers our life.
Recently, because of the flu spread, many people are taking cold medicine.
For this reasons, I want to make a site that tells people how to use the medicine correctly.


Purpose of Website

  • Understand basic concepts of medicine.
  • Understand the working principles and processes of medicine.
  • Learn about medicine manual, and know what to be careful.
  • Know how to have medicine correctly.
